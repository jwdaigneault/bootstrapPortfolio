Who Are We?

New England Excursions offers short-term getaways in the great outdoors while meeting like minded adventurers to escape into the wilderness and disconnect! Whether you enjoy hiking or spending time out on the water, New England Excursions offers something for everyone. You can choose between day tours, including kayaking, cycling, and hiking trips, to overnight or multinight stays. We handle all of the details and planning, so you can focus on the day ahead!
